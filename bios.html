Kelly McGee
Senior | Chapter President
Major: 2A
Favorite Quote: "Not all heroes wear capes"
Background
I'm a junior studying Mechanical Engineering here at MIT concentrating in Product Development. I'm originally from Pasadena, California and if there is anything that the cold Boston winters have taught me, it's that I have to move back to California after I graduate. Luckily, I have had the opportunity to live in the Bay Area for 6 months of my Junior year as an intern at Apple in Cupertino. I work as a Product Design Engineer in the Packaging Product Design group, working on Apple Watch products. Growing up, I've always been a big outdoorsman. I've traveled the world backpacking, taking me everywhere from summiting the Grand Tetons in Wyoming to climbing the Alps and Pyrenees. I'm sort of a water creature, as I play on the Mens Varsity Water Polo team and try to spend more time in the water surfing than on land when I get to visit home. I'm an active member of the Undergraduate Association on campus as Chair of the Special Projects Committee, where I manage a group of undergraduates to develop new ideas to improve student life at MIT. This past semester we've focused on mental health awareness, bringing administrators and students together for town hall meetings, as well as on food, attempting to bring a diverse selection of Food Trucks to the MIT community. I've UROPedin the Media Lab for two years, working on creating smarter homes in the CityHome project. Specifically I was helping to design pneumatic actuators that can move furniture and walls around your home to make spaces in metropolitan cities more efficient. I also am working on a startup with a group out of San Diego focusing on drone defense technology. Lastly, I freelance as a graphic designer and web developer, helping MIT and local startups with their brand design and web / mobile presence. And when I'm not doing all this other stuff, I try to find time to travel with my girlfriend, get this fraternity up and running, and rock climb.
